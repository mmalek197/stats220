My YouTube Video Log

This report looks at data collected from a Google Form about YouTube videos I watch. The form logs the category of the video, how long it was, how I found it, and whether I watched the whole thing.

I have logged 22 videos so far. The average video length was 17.5 minutes, with the shortest being 3 minutes and the longest being 60 minutes.

The bar chart above shows the categories of videos I watched. Gaming and Music seem to come up the most which makes sense tbh.

This chart shows how I found the videos. A lot of them were either recommended by YouTube or through my subscriptions which is pretty typical.
